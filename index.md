Privacy Policy for SimpleRead

Last Updated: 17/03/2026

Data Collection and Usage
SimpleRead is a Chrome Extension designed to simplify hard-to-read web text into plain English, directly on the page you are reading.

Local Processing Only
All text processing happens entirely on your device using Chrome's built-in on-device AI (Gemini Nano). No text you select or simplify is sent anywhere.

No Data Transmission
We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal data, browsing activity, or page content with any external servers or third parties. Your data never leaves your browser.

Contact
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact get.simpleread@gmail.com
